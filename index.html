History

Secret Santa itself originated a bit later...

The tradition of giving anonymous gifts at Christmas is originally from Scandinavia. Julklapp is a word which roughly translates to Christmas knock. The tradition is that you should knock loudly on a door, open it and leave the gift behind without being seen by anyone. This is a custom based loosely on the idea of Servant Rupert who wanders from house to house giving out gifts to serve his master Santa Claus

It also has it's origins in Germanic Christmas traditions where Witchel or helper fairies would help Santa in the deliverance of gifts

In the modern day Secret Santa is a cost-effective way of telling large groups of people you care about them. It also helps Santa out as climate change is reducing the size of his toy making factories each year
